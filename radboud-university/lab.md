## Description

The Donders Institute is one of Europe's leading neuroscience centers, conducting fundamental, cognitive, and clinical neuroscience research. The institute brings together researchers from multiple disciplines to understand the mechanistic, computational, and dynamic underpinnings of cognition and behavior in health and disease.

The Donders Institute is renowned for its integrative approach, combining neuroimaging (MRI, MEG, EEG), computational modeling, genetics, and clinical research to address fundamental questions about brain function.

## Research Focus

- **Cognitive Neuroscience** - Attention, memory, language, decision-making
- **Systems Neuroscience** - Neural circuits, population coding, brain dynamics
- **Computational Neuroscience** - Neural modeling, machine learning, predictive coding
- **Clinical Neuroscience** - Neurological disorders, psychiatric conditions
- **Neuroimaging Methods** - fMRI, MEG, EEG, multimodal integration
- **Social Neuroscience** - Communication, social cognition, interaction

## Research Themes

1. **Language and Communication**
2. **Perception, Action and Control**
