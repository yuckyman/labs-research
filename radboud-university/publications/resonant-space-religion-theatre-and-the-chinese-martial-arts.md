# Resonant space: religion, theatre, and the Chinese martial arts

## Abstract

<jats:p>Resonant Space constellates the martial, ritual, and theatrical elements of the Chinese martial arts with the practice of contemporary theatre and dance. This interdisciplinary approach blends the embodied experiences of the author, a lifelong student of the Chinese martial arts and a theatre director and dance dramaturg, with the study of Chinese cultural history. This is a work for scholars and practitioners of the Chinese martial arts, of contemporary dance and theatre, and for scholars of Chinese religion and cultural history.
The book includes prefaces by Gey Pin Ang and Peter Lorge, a chapter by Colin Lalonde, and a postface by Guy Cools.</jats:p>
