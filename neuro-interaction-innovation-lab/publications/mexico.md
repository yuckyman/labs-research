# Mexico

## Abstract

<jats:title>Abstract</jats:title>
                  <jats:p>This chapter overviews the situation of children and youth who are institutionalized or in the process of deinstitutionalization and leaving care in Mexico, according to data obtained from various government institutions: municipal, state, and federal, as well as nonprofit organizations up to 2024. The purpose is to understand the current situation regarding the legal and political position in which children and youth find themselves throughout their placement trajectory while paying particular attention to their process of leaving care. Overall, Mexico is struggling to reform its care systems and to date has yet much to do to develop policies or programs to facilitate the transition to adulthood from care.</jats:p>
