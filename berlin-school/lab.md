## Description

The Berlin School of Mind and Brain is an interdisciplinary graduate school focused on the intersection of neuroscience, psychology, philosophy, and cognitive science. The school brings together researchers and students from multiple institutions in Berlin to investigate the mind-brain relationship through diverse methodological approaches.
