## Description

The Bernstein Center for Computational Neuroscience (BCCN) is a leading research center focused on computational neuroscience, combining theoretical and experimental approaches to understand brain function. The center brings together researchers from multiple institutions in Berlin to investigate neural information processing, brain-computer interfaces, and computational modeling of neural systems.

Research at BCCN spans from single neurons to complex cognitive processes, utilizing advanced neuroimaging, electrophysiology, and computational methods.

## Research Focus

- **Computational Neuroscience** - Neural network models, information processing
- **Brain-Computer Interfaces** - Neurotechnology, neural signal processing
- **Brain Simulation** - Large-scale neural modeling, The Virtual Brain
- **Neuroimaging** - fMRI decoding, neural signal analysis
- **Visual and Motor Systems** - Perception, action, sensorimotor integration
- **Computational Modeling in Medicine** - Clinical applications of computational neuroscience
